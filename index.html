Ecuadorian Shrimp Ceviche Recipe

Preparation time: 30 minutes

Ceviche is a popular seafood dish in Ecuador, especially made with fresh shrimp. This refreshing and tangy meal is perfect for hot days or as an appetizer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1/2 cup orange juice for a sweeter touch

Instructions:

  1. Boil the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes until pink. Drain and let them cool.
  2. In a bowl, mix the shrimp with lime juice and let it marinate for 10 minutes.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the onion, tomato, cilantro, and bell pepper.
  4. Add the marinated shrimp to the vegetable mixture. Stir gently to combine.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Add orange juice for a touch of sweetness if desired.
  6. Serve chilled with plantain chips or toasted corn.
"The perfect balance of flavors!" - Chef Cristian
