Ecuadorian Ceviche Recipe

Preparation time: 30 minutes

Ceviche is a popular seafood dish in Ecuador, known for its refreshing and tangy flavor. It's perfect for a light meal or appetizer.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb fresh fish (corvina or any white fish), cubed
  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1/2 cup orange juice for a sweeter touch

Instructions:

  1. Place the fish in a bowl and cover with lime juice. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for about 15 minutes, or until the fish is opaque.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix the onion, tomato, cilantro, and green bell pepper.
  3. Add the marinated fish to the vegetable mix, and stir gently to combine.
  4. Drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. If desired, add orange juice for a sweeter flavor. Serve chilled.
"The perfect summer dish!" - Chef Juan, 2024

Enjoy your homemade Ecuadorian ceviche with some plantain chips or toasted corn for an authentic experience!
